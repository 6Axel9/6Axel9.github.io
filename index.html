PROJECT_04 NOV 2023

Noun Town

  • Unity
  • C#
  • Cross-Platform
  • Learning

A language learning game where mastering vocabulary in VR, MR, or desktop brings a greyscale island back to life. Here I focused on shading work and minigame creation later on becoming the lead of the VR and MR part of the experience. Database, saves and cache management are also a major part of what made this project challening as it currently feature full cross-progression compatibility across all of it's release and loads of different environments to play in.
